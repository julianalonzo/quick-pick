About QuickPick

Do you feel like you are always short on time during lunch breaks? Have you experienced ordering something you did not like just because you had no choice? Are you tired of eating at the same place everyday?

QuickPick is the solution you need!

QuickPick is an online food menu that displays ahead of time the daily food servings of the selected food places near the SLU Maryheights Campus (e.g., 1st floor canteen, 3rd floor canteen, Teacup, etc.)

It aims to assist SABM and SCIS students and professors to easily plan their meals for tomorrow.
